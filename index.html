Volcanoes!

Introduction:

Volcanoes, who doesn't love them? (Assuming you're not too close to them of course). They're the most explosive things on Earth, even beating out Nuclear Bombs. The TSAR bomb for example, the most powerful nuclear weapon ever created, was detonated in 1961, with an explosive yield equal to 50 MEGATONS of TNT. The flare (flash), from the explosion was visible from over 1,000 kmaway, and the blast wave circled around the ENTIRETY OF EARTH, THREE TIMES, glass windows even shattered 780 kilometers away. The most powerful volcanic eruption in RECENT history (past few thousand years), on the other hand, was the 1815 eruption of Mount Tambora, and had a yield of approx 33 GIGATONS of TNT. That is roughly FIVE HUNDRED TSAR bombs. Roughly 100 cubic kilometers of rock was blasted into the air. So much ash lingered in the air that the global temperature dropped by 0.4 to 0.7 Celsius, for about A DECADE.
